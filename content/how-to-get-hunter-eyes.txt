# How to Get Hunter Eyes – Canthal Tilt, Hooded Lids, and Eye Looksmaxxing

"Hunter eyes" is one of the most discussed concepts in looksmaxxing. It refers to a specific eye shape that's consistently rated as more attractive and masculine — characterized by a positive canthal tilt, slight hooding, and deep orbital setting.

Here's what determines it, what you can change, and how to maximize your eye aesthetics.

---

## What Are Hunter Eyes?

Hunter eyes are defined by:

1. **Positive canthal tilt** — The outer corner of the eye is higher than the inner corner, creating an upward slant
2. **Hooded lids** — The orbital bone creates a slight overhang, giving a deep, intense appearance
3. **Deep-set eyes** — Eyes that sit back in the skull, creating shadow and depth
4. **Compact, almond shape** — Not wide, round, or bulging

This is opposed to "prey eyes" — wide, round, bulging eyes with a negative canthal tilt (outer corner lower than inner).

---

## What Determines Your Canthal Tilt?

Canthal tilt is primarily determined by:

**1. Orbital bone structure** — The shape of the eye socket is largely genetic. The position of the lateral orbital rim (the bone at the outer corner) determines the tilt.

**2. Midface structure** — Forward malar (cheekbone) projection supports the outer eye corner and creates positive tilt.

**3. Fat distribution** — Fat around the eye area affects how the lid sits.

**4. Age** — Canthal tilt degrades with age as tissue loosens.

---

## What Can You Change?

### Face Fat Reduction (High Impact)

Excess fat around the eyes creates a rounder, more "prey" appearance. Losing face fat:
- Reduces undereye puffiness
- Creates more defined orbital area
- Can reveal underlying positive canthal tilt hidden by fat

Get to 12–15% body fat and reassess your eye shape.

### Mewing and Midface Development (Long-Term)

Correct tongue posture and forward facial development through mewing can improve midface structure over years, which indirectly affects how the outer eye corner sits.

### Sleep Optimization

Poor sleep causes under-eye puffiness and dark circles — two of the most visually damaging things for eye aesthetics. 8+ hours dramatically changes how your eyes look.

### Skincare for the Eye Area

- Vitamin C serum reduces dark circles
- Retinol firms the skin around the eyes
- SPF prevents UV-induced skin loosening

### Eye Area Grooming

- Clean up your eyebrows — don't over-pluck, but remove strays
- Thick, well-shaped brows frame the eyes and enhance the hunter eye appearance
- Avoid bushy, unkempt brows that draw attention downward

---

## Surgical Options (Not Recommended as First Step)

Advanced looksmaxxers sometimes discuss:
- **Canthoplasty** — Surgical repositioning of the outer eye corner for positive canthal tilt
- **Orbital rim implants** — Creates deeper set appearance

These are significant surgeries with real risks. Exhaust all softmaxxing options first.
