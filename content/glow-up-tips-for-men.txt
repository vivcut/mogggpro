# 10 Glow Up Tips for Men That Actually Work

A "glow up" isn't magic. It's a series of deliberate, stacked improvements that compound into a dramatically better version of yourself. Here are the 10 highest-ROI changes any man can make.

---

## 1. Fix Your Sleep First

Every other improvement is limited by your sleep quality. 7–9 hours of quality sleep:
- Releases HGH (repairs and builds everything)
- Reduces cortisol (less facial puffiness)
- Clears dark circles
- Improves skin clarity

Sleep before midnight. Dark, cold room. No screens 1 hour before bed.

---

## 2. Cut Alcohol and Reduce Sodium

Alcohol and excess sodium are the two fastest ways to destroy your facial definition. Both cause water retention and puffiness.

Cut both for 2 weeks. Take before/after photos. The difference will shock you.

---

## 3. Start a Basic Skincare Routine

3 products, 3 minutes, morning and evening:
- **Gentle cleanser** (Cerave)
- **SPF 30+ sunscreen** (morning only — non-negotiable)
- **Moisturizer**

Add retinol after 4 weeks once your barrier is stable. Clear skin is one of the most impactful changes you can make.

---

## 4. Get a Proper Haircut

Most men are walking around with completely wrong haircuts for their face shape. A skilled barber who understands facial structure can transform your appearance in 30 minutes.

**Generally works well:**
- Tapered or faded sides (creates illusion of height and jawline)
- Texture on top
- Styled, not flat

Tell your barber: "I want something that enhances my jawline and makes my face look more defined."

---

## 5. Go to the Gym

Building muscle changes your entire silhouette and hormonal profile. Priority muscles for aesthetics:
- **Shoulders/delts** — Creates the V-taper, makes you look powerful
- **Lats** — Widens your back, creates V-taper
- **Neck** — Often ignored but enormous for masculine appearance
- **Glutes** — Improves posture and overall frame

Aim for 3–4 strength training sessions per week.

---

## 6. Lose the Face Fat

Body fat accumulates on the face visibly. Even 10–15 lbs of weight loss dramatically reshapes cheekbones, jawline, and overall facial structure.

Target: 12–15% body fat for men to reveal bone structure.

---

## 7. Fix Your Posture

Forward head posture and rounded shoulders make you look smaller, less confident, and hides your jaw. This is fixable.

Daily: chin tucks, wall slides, dead hangs. Your posture communicates before you speak.

---

## 8. Upgrade Your Style

Clothing that fits well is an instant glow up. Rules:
- Nothing baggy — fitted silhouettes only
- Monochromatic fits look cleaner and taller
- Neutral colors (black, white, grey, navy) for everyday
- Invest in 3–5 quality basics over 20 cheap items

---

## 9. Improve Your Grooming

Eyebrows, body hair, facial hair — all matter.
- Clean up your eyebrows (don't over-pluck — just remove strays)
- Keep facial hair intentional and shaped
- Trim body hair
- Get a beard oil or face moisturizer for beard skin

---

## 10. Start Mewing and Jaw Exercises

For long-term structural improvement:
- Correct tongue posture (mewing) daily
- Chew mastic gum 20–30 min/day
- These compound over months and years
