# What Is Looksmaxxing? The Complete Beginner's Guide

Looksmaxxing is the practice of maximizing your physical attractiveness using a combination of grooming, fitness, skincare, style, and behavioral techniques. It's not about being vain — it's about optimizing your appearance to perform at your genetic best.

The term comes from online communities where people study facial aesthetics, bone structure, and the science of attraction to systematically improve their looks.

---

## Why Does It Matter?

Research consistently shows that physical attractiveness influences:
- Social outcomes and first impressions
- Dating and relationship success
- Career advancement and salary
- Self-confidence and mental health

Looksmaxxing isn't about becoming someone you're not. It's about removing obstacles between who you are and how attractive you can be.

---

## The Two Categories of Looksmaxxing

### Softmaxxing (Things You Can Change Now)

Softmaxxing refers to improvements that don't involve permanent changes:

- **Skincare** — Clear skin can add 1-2 points to your perceived attractiveness
- **Haircut** — The right haircut can dramatically change your face shape perception
- **Grooming** — Eyebrow shaping, body hair, facial hair
- **Body composition** — Losing fat and building muscle reshapes your face and frame
- **Style** — Clothing that fits well and suits your aesthetics
- **Sleep** — 8 hours of quality sleep shows on your face immediately
- **Posture** — Standing straight can add 1-2 inches of perceived height

### Hardmaxxing (Structural Changes)

Hardmaxxing refers to more permanent changes:

- **Mewing** — Correct tongue posture that can reshape the palate and jaw over years
- **Jaw exercises** — Mastic gum, jawline exercisers
- **Rhinoplasty, jaw surgery** — Surgical options (not recommended as a starting point)
- **Orthodontics** — Fixing bite alignment

## The Looksmaxxing Hierarchy (Prioritize This Order)

1. **Sleep** — Free, immediate impact
2. **Diet** — Reduce facial bloat, improve skin
3. **Skincare routine** — 5 minutes a day, huge ROI
4. **Body composition** — Gym + diet
5. **Haircut + grooming** — Once a month
6. **Style** — One-time investment
7. **Mewing + jaw exercises** — Long-term structural change

## How Long Does Looksmaxxing Take?

Quick wins (1–2 weeks):
- Cut sodium and alcohol → face debloats
- Better sleep → under-eye circles improve
- New haircut → face shape perception changes

Medium-term (1–6 months):
- Skincare → skin clarity and texture
- Weight loss → face fat reduction
- Gym → overall physique

Long-term (6–24 months):
- Mewing → jaw and facial structure
- Body transformation → significant recomposition

## The PSL Rating System

Looksmaxxers use the PSL scale (1–8) to objectively rate facial attractiveness. Here's the breakdown:

- **1.0–4.0** — Below average to average
- **4.0–5.0** — Mid-Tier Normie (the average person)
- **5.0–6.0** — High-Tier Normie (conventionally attractive)
- **6.0–7.0** — Chadlite to Chad (top 2–15%)
- **7.0–8.0** — Giga Chad to Tera Chad (top 0.1%)

Most people who start looksmaxxing can realistically move up 0.5–1.5 PSL points through softmaxxing alone.
